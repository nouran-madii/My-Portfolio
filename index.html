About Me

I’m a creative Frontend Developer, Web Designer, and LEGO Robotics Instructor who loves bringing ideas to life — designing modern, responsive websites and teaching robotics through fun, hands-on LEGO projects. Whether it’s crafting smooth, interactive web experiences or guiding students through their first robot build, I’m all about making technology exciting, simple, and meaningful.
